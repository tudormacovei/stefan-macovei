Biography

From Nicosia
to Copenhagen

Ștefan Macovei was born in 1999 in Nicosia, Cyprus. He completed his Bachelor's degree at the "Gheorghe Dima" National Academy of Music in Cluj-Napoca, Romania, and continued his studies at the Accademia del Ridotto in Italy under Prof. Vincenzo Balzani — a collaboration that began in 2016.

Since 2023, he has been pursuing his Master's degree at the Royal Danish Academy of Music in Copenhagen, in the class of Prof. Marianna Shirinyan. He has performed across Europe, in Romania, Cyprus, Italy, Denmark, Germany, France, Spain, Sweden, Switzerland, Lithuania, Malta, and Russia.
